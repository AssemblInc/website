ABOUT

What we do:

Assembl is working to solve problems that inhibit the advancement of scientific research. Our current mission is to improve the quality and accessibility of scientific data by interconnecting disparate data sources and positively incentivizing data interchange.

Who we are:

Assembl was co-founded by Sebastian Mellen and Freek Bes. We have grown into a small team of programmers, scientists, and researchers who envision a world of unbounded scientific exploration, free from corporate control and stagnating centralization. If you are interested in joining our team, please send us an e-mail at team@assembl.science or submit your CV and email here.

For scientists:

If you are a forward-thinking scientist, independent researcher, or member of an academic institution interested in a secure, simple, and unhampered method of exchanging data, please send us an e-mail at outreach@assembl.science or sign-up for our alpha deployment here.
