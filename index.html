About Assembl

Assembl is working to solve problems that inhibit the advancement of scientific research. Our current mission is to improve the quality and accessibility of scientific data by interconnecting disparate data sources and positively incentivizing data interchange.

Assembl was co-founded by Sebastian Mellen and Freek Bes. We have grown into a small team of programmers, scientists, and researchers who envision a world of unbounded scientific exploration, free from corporate control and stagnating centralization.
