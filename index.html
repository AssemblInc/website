About Assembl

Since our founding in March of 2018, we've been solving problems that inhibit the advancement of scientific research. Our mission is to improve the quality and accessibility of scientific data with simple to use tools. Our application simplifies the process of exchanging data and incentivizes data sharing with a unique licensing system.

Assembl was founded by Sebastian Mellen and Freek Bes. We've grown into a team of scientists, programmers, and researchers who envision a world of unbounded scientific exploration, free from corporate control and stagnating centralization. Through the development of simple tools, we can tackle complex problems and bring this vision to reality. If our vision inspires you as well, please spread the word. You can follow us on Twitter, Mastodon, and Minds, or read our blog at assembl.ch/blog.
